Cultural Event Call for Proposals - Example
CALL FOR PROPOSALS No. 01/2026 - CULTURAL PROJECT SELECTION

1. Purpose
1.1. This call aims to select cultural projects for events in the categories: concerts, theater, film screenings, and musicals.

2. Goals
2.1. Promote local cultural production.
2.2. Encourage inclusion and democratized access to culture.

3. Eligibility
3.1. Individuals over 18 years old and legal entities with proven cultural activity may apply.
3.2. Each applicant may submit up to 2 projects.

4. Submission period
4.1. Start: June 10, 2026
4.2. End: July 10, 2026 at 11:59 PM
4.3. Submissions outside the deadline will be disqualified.

5. Required documents
5.1. Completed submission form.
5.2. Identification document of the responsible person.
5.3. Proof of residence or business address.
5.4. Short portfolio.
5.5. Execution plan and detailed budget.

6. Evaluation criteria
6.1. Cultural impact (weight 30%).
6.2. Technical and financial feasibility (weight 30%).
6.3. Audience reach (weight 20%).
6.4. Inclusion and accessibility (weight 20%).

7. Selection stages
7.1. Document screening.
7.2. Technical evaluation by committee.
7.3. Final ranking by score.
7.4. Approval and publication of final results.

8. Results
8.1. Preliminary results will be published on the official portal.
8.2. A 3-business-day appeal window will be granted, according to published rules.

9. Final provisions
9.1. Submission implies full awareness and acceptance of this call's rules.
9.2. Omitted cases will be decided by the organizing committee.
