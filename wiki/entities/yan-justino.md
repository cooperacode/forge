Yan Justino is a Staff Software Engineer at Banco Itaú and researcher at CESAR School. He is the creator of **forge** and its direct predecessor, [protocolo-es-ai](protocolo-es-ai.md).

His work spans two parallel tracks: academic research in legacy system reengineering, service-oriented architecture, and DevOps process design — published at ICSE (2018), SOCA (2021–2022), and IEEE (2022) — and hands-on leadership of large-scale modernization programs at Itaú Unibanco.

forge is the convergence of both tracks: the theoretical foundation comes from the [SPReaD](spread-methodology.md) methodology (da Silva, Justino & Adachi, SOCA 2022), while the industry scope and practical validation come from the transformation programs he led at Itaú.
